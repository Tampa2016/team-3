About Us


This website and its companion iOS application strive to improve the quality of life for persons living with physical

disabilities. This is accomplished through an application that motivates individuals (both those living with and without

paralysis) to report on inaccessible places. Users are awarded points for providing images and reviews of various

locations and for assessing the credibility of the reviews made by other usersUsers can either redeem points for gift cards

provided by corporate sponsors of Yada or choose to have money donated to charity in their name. Companies could use this

app to run corporate social responsibility programs with their employees.


By bringing a stronger awareness of these barriers to the general public, we are one step closer to eliminating such obstacles.

What it Does


This app allows users to upload photos and written reviews about the accessibility of particular businesses and locations.

Users can also use the "Explore" function to find nearby locations in a particular category ranked based on a combination of

their proximity, Yelp scores, and the ADA accesibility scores that are calculated from the user reviews.
